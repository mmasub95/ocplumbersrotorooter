Aging Pipes in Orange County

Many Orange County homes — particularly those built before 1980 — still have galvanized steel or cast iron pipes. These materials corrode from the inside out, causing reduced water pressure, rust-colored water, and eventually leaks. We offer full repipe services using modern copper or PEX piping for long-term reliability.

Sewer Line Repair & Replacement

Sewer line problems are serious — and often invisible until a backup or collapse occurs. We use video camera inspection to pinpoint the exact location and condition of your sewer line before recommending repair or replacement. Where possible, we use trenchless methods to minimize digging in your yard.

Signs Your Pipes Need Repair

  • Visible rust stains or moisture under sinks or on ceilings
  • Unexplained increase in water bills (indicating a hidden leak)
  • Low water pressure throughout the home
  • Discolored water (rust or brown tint) from hot or cold taps
  • Recurring drain backups or slow drains in multiple fixtures
  • Wet spots in the yard or foundation cracking (possible sewer issue)
